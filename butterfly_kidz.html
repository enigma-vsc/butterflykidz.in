ABOUT US

A Place Where Children Truly Blossom

At Butterfly Kidz Pre School, we believe every child is unique and full of potential. Our preschool provides a safe, happy, and nurturing environment where children learn through play, exploration, and creativity.

We focus on building strong foundations in learning, confidence, and social skills during the most important early years of your child's life.

OUR PURPOSE

Our Vision & Mission

The values and commitments that guide everything we do at Butterfly Kidz.

1
🌟 Our Vision

At Butterfly Kidz Pre School, our vision is to nurture joyful, confident, and curious children by building a strong foundation for lifelong learning. We imagine every child blossoming into their fullest potential — just like a caterpillar transforms into a beautiful butterfly.

2
💛 Our Mission

Our mission is to create a safe and joyful environment where children feel secure, loved, and free to be themselves. We are committed to the holistic development of every child — supporting emotional, social, physical, and cognitive growth through play-based learning, creative activities, and interactive experiences that help children develop confidence, communication skills, and independence at their own pace.

PROGRAMS OFFERED

The Right Program for Every Little Learner

Age-appropriate programs designed to grow with your child from their very first school experience.

1
🧸
Playgroup
Age: 2+ years

Learning basic words, rhymes, colors, shapes, and social habits through play and interaction in a warm and welcoming setting.

2
🌱
Nursery
Age: 3+ years

Introduction to alphabets, numbers, colors, shapes, rhymes, and creative activities to build early learning skills with confidence.

3
📚
LKG
Age: 4+ years

Learning phonics, letter writing, number concepts, vocabulary building, and fine motor skills through structured and engaging activities.

4
🎓
UKG
Age: 5+ years

Developing reading, writing, basic math, sentence formation, and school readiness skills to prepare confident young learners.
